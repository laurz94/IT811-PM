Project Execution

Steps for Project Execution

  1. Development Team will be outsourced to a local firm.
  2. Directors and Management team will assign resources.
  3. Management team will procure resources according the procurement plan.
  4. Business Analyst will create user stories.
  5. The team will work scrum with 2 week sprints.
  6. Teams will meet for daily standup at 9am to give status updates.
  7. Project Manager will send updates to stakeholders at the end of each sprint.
  8. Project Manager will review and update the project schedule and plan at the end of each sprint.
